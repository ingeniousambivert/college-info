    • BCA

    The BCA is an undergraduate degree course in computer applications for duration of 3 years. After completing BCA, a student can go for MCA which is a master course in computer application and is considered equivalent to engineering course (B.Tech).

    iMSCIT

    iMSc(IT) is a full time, five year (ten semesters) course, intended for students seeking a professional career in the IT industry. The programme aims to indoctrinate crucial abilities as demanded by the industry through interactive learning process.
