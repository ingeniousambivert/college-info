    Learn More About Bachelor of Computer Application

    GLS University at the time of its inception established Faculty of Computer Applications and Information Technology, with the objective of imparting quality education at under graduate and post graduate level in the field of Computer Applications and Information Technology. With rapidly evolving technology and the continuous need for innovation the department has always produced quality professionals. Bachelor of Computer Applications programme was established in the year 1999 with the vision to be a pioneer institute that prepares skilled students under the umbrella of Gujarat Law Society (GLS). Currently, the institute flares its wings and touches new horizons of excellence under Faculty of Computer Applications & Information Technology, GLS University. It has become a well reputed institution with great level of excellence in academic circuits. Since its inception, the institute started gaining reputation for its excellent teaching mechanism and its student oriented approaches. Over the years, the institute has become a synonymous with practical, industry-focused education and attracts the best students from across the country. The institute also lays strong emphasis on social values, so that the student emerge as a successful and ethical professional as well as a responsible human being.

    The Institute follows several best practices that include
